Car Description

The Maruti 800, launched in 1983, is one of India's most iconic hatchbacks. Renowned for its affordability, reliability, and fuel efficiency, the Maruti 800 revolutionized personal transportation in the country. Powered by a 796cc engine, it offers a nimble and smooth driving experience, making it an all-time favorite.
